Event Info

We politely request a dress code of smart casual and up. This means you can wear a plain tee, jeans, and a nice pair of shoes, all the way up to a full tux if you want.

The ceremony and dinner will be outdoors, weather permitting, while cocktail hour and the dance floor will be indoors.

Ceremony 5:00pm EDT

Please arrive 15 minutes early. We will have pre-ceremony refreshments, maybe, hopefully.

Cocktail Hour 5:30pm EDT

Please enjoy our passed hors d'œuvres and open bar. There will be a photobooth station at this time, please sign our guest book photo album!

Reception 7:00pm EDT - midnight

Buffet-style dinner will be served, and we will perform a tea ceremony. After that, there will be a bouquet Pusheen toss and an open dance floor to follow!

活動資訊

我們謹請所有參加者穿著得體的休閒服裝或更正式的服裝。這意味著您可以穿一件淨色的T恤、牛仔褲和一雙漂亮的鞋子、也可以穿一套正式的西裝禮服。

如果天氣允許、儀式和晚餐將在室外、而鷄尾酒時間和舞池則在室內。

儀式 紐約時間下午5:00

請提前15分鐘到達。我們可能會提供儀式前的茶點。

鷄尾酒時間 紐約時間下午5:30

請享受我們供應的點心和開放式酒吧。屆時將有攝影亭、請在我們的來賓留言簿上簽名！

婚宴 紐約時間晚上7:00 - 半夜

我們將提供自助餐式晚餐、也會舉行敬茶儀式。之後、將會抛手捧花 Pusheen 和開放舞池！
